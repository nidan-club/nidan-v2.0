What is Nidan Card?

Nidan card is a RFID health card used to store an individual's medical history and important information such as allergies, medical conditions, medications, and previous surgeries. In an emergency, healthcare providers can access the card's information immediately, allowing them to make quick and informed decisions about treatment. By carrying a Nidan card, individuals can ensure that they receive prompt and appropriate medical care in any situation.
